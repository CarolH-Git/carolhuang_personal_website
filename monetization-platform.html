Creator Monetization Platform

A hybrid CPC/CPM monetization system that connected local businesses with relevant creators, ensuring fair compensation and measurable results for all stakeholders.

Problem

Creators were struggling with inconsistent monetization models, while businesses faced challenges in connecting with relevant content producers. The existing systems either favored established creators or provided unpredictable income, creating barriers to sustainable content creation.

Solution

Developed a hybrid CPC/CPM monetization platform that intelligently matched local businesses with creators whose audiences aligned with their target demographics. The system provided creators with reliable income streams while giving businesses measurable ROI on their marketing investments.

My Role

Led the cross-functional project team and orchestrated collaboration between the platform and three vertical business units, driving product strategy from concept to successful implementation.

Results

  • Significantly enhanced creator revenue by implementing a hybrid monetization model
  • Attracted more high-quality creators to the platform
  • Increased high-quality content production
  • Improved advertiser satisfaction with measurable ROI
